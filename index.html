I am a researcher at Istituto di Matematica Applicata e Tecnologie Informatiche "Enrico Magenes" (IMATI) of the National Research Council of Italy (CNR). Here you can find some information about me and my research activities.

Current projects I am member of

  • RAISE: part of the post COVID pandemic Piano Nazionale di Rinascita e Resilienza (PNRR)collaborating with Domenico Federico Gallipoli Leonardo Gatti Simone Lalicata Lorenzo Pittaluga Tamellini on the coupling of Richards' equation with limit equilibrium models to assess the risk of rainfall-induced land slides.
  • COSMIC (PRIN No. 2022A79M75): a national PRIN2022 project on isogeometric analysis in methods collaboration with Cesare Carlotta Bracco Giannelli (PI)Alessandro Reale.
  • PRIN-PNRR (P2022LXLYY): a national project on uncertainty quantification for coupled models with emphasis on fluid flows in porous poroelasticity media and transport of collaboration contaminants with Francesca Sauro Locatelli Lorenzo Manenti Tamellini (PI)Sara Pietro Todeschini Zanotti.

Previous projects I have been involved in

  • CE4a WE project from the Lombardia administrative region about modeling the groundwater flow in the Ticino river valley.
  • FP7 project FEEC-A as about PostDoc applications of Finete Element Exterior Calculus under the supervision of Ragnar Winther.
  • EXAMPLE as a Marie-Curie Fellow under the supervision of Bert Jüttler.
  • FWF-NFN project Geometry + simulationa project about IsoGeometric Analysis whose main product is the G+SMO software library
  • FP7 projects TERRIFIC
