Current projects I am member of

  • RAISE: part of the post COVID pandemic Piano Nazionale di Rinascita e Resilienza (PNRR) collaborating with Domenico Gallipoli, Federico Gatti, Leonardo Lalicata, Simone Pittaluga and Lorenzo Tamellini on the coupling of Richards' equation with limit equilibrium models to assess the risk of rainfall-induced land slides.
  • COSMIC (PRIN No. 2022A79M75): a national PRIN2022 project on isogeometric analysis in collaboration with Cesare Bracco, Carlotta Giannelli (PI), Alessandro Reali and collaborators.
  • PRIN-PNRR (P2022LXLYY): a national project on uncertainty quantification for coupled models with emphasis on fluid flows in porous media, poroelasticity and transport of contaminants in collaboration with Francesca Locatelli, Sauro Manenti, Lorenzo Tamellini (PI), Sara Todeschini and Pietro Zanotti.

Previous projects I have been involved in

  • CE4WE project from the Lombardia administrative region about modeling the groundwater flow in the Ticino river valley.
  • FP7 project FEEC-A as about PostDoc applications of Finete Element Exterior Calculus under the supervision of Ragnar Winther.
  • EXAMPLE as a Marie-Curie Fellow under the supervision of Bert Jüttler.
  • FWF-NFN project Geometry + simulationa project about IsoGeometric Analysis whose main product is the G+SMO software library
  • FP7 projects TERRIFIC
